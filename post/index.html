Visualisation

When I was young, I didn’t give a shi* about presentation. I recall a school project, where the evaluation matrix specifically said presentation would make up 50% of the score. I think I only presented numbers and text. And gave up 50% of the score. Fast forward 20 years, and I’m now wiser to a truth that I had no inkling of as a child: humans are visual animals.
